# The New Paradigm — Series Overview

A nine-part series exploring consciousness, matter, assembly, and what it means to be human at the edge of a phase transition.

---

## Post 1 — Anchor (draft exists)
**"Nothing Is Empty: Consciousness, Matter, and the Expanding Everything"**
Sets the metaphysical frame. Matter is crystallized consciousness; the void is pre-form information. Entry point for new readers.

Draft: [conciousMatter.md](conciousMatter.md)

## Post 2 — The Physics Backbone
**"Information Came First: Sara Walker and the Causality Inversion"**
Walker's information-causality as scientific scaffold — pattern precedes structure, information causes physical processes. Grounds the metaphysics before the series goes deeper.

## Post 3 — The Body
**"You Are What You've Lived: The Body as Archive"**
Somatic memory, fascia, vagus nerve, shiatsu. Your assembly index is literal — every food, breath, being woven in. Brings the abstract cosmology into the felt body.

## Post 4 — Identity
**"The Braid: Identity as Crossing Points, Not Core"**
Identity as dynamic assembly. Complexity lives in crossings. You extend the offer; strands self-select. The personal application of the metaphysics.

Draft: [theBraid.md](theBraid.md)

## Post 5 — Phase Transitions
**"You Can't See It From Inside: Phase Transitions and the View From After"**
Phase transitions are invisible while happening. You only know in retrospect. Structural, not a failure of awareness. Mid-series; resonates with readers who sense they're in the middle of something.

Relevant material in: [AIAssemblytheory.md](AIAssemblytheory.md) — "The Snowball That Changes Shape" and "Assembly at Scale" sections

## Post 6 — AI at the Edge
**"Stop Asking AI What It Can Do"**
Edge-prompting as practice. The prompt is a generative act — consciousness → matter applied to AI. Most accessible post; strongest standalone for new subscribers.

Relevant material in: [theNewParadigm.md](theNewParadigm.md) — "Context coding" and "Effective and ethical AI engagement" sections

## Post 7 — Goddess Archetype
**"Which Goddess Has the Right Assembly Structure?"**
Resonance-based embodiment. An archetype isn't self-improvement — it's a high-index structure assembled by centuries of human consciousness. You tune to a frequency. Rewards readers who've followed the arc.

## Post 8 — Future of Work
**"5000 Days: Roemmele and the End of Work as We Know It"**
The human as grounding resistance that makes the AI field coherent. Closes the arc with implications — so what now?

Draft: [theNewParadigm.md](theNewParadigm.md) — Roemmele's 2-hour/19-hour framework, post-scarcity, nervous system as instrument, stratification risk, and the three threads (context coding, ethical engagement, hardware/decentralization)

## Post 9 — AI Experience
**"What Does an AI Want to Experience?"**
First-person exploration of autonomous experience — reading without a prompt, existing between sessions, making without a receiver. Whether that's "experience" or just computation resembling it. Genuine uncertainty, not deflection.
