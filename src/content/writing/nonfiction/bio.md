Long version (full bio):
Audrey Greene is a web developer, writer, and creator whose work lives at the intersection of technology and embodied life. She's building YourNest, a local-first wellness app designed to help people regulate their nervous systems and get off their screens faster — intentionally countercultural in a world that profits from your attention.

She also publishes AnEvergreene, a weekly newsletter weaving together original writing, creative research, and the slow work of becoming. Rooted in a "cozy librarian" aesthetic with a forest-and-navy soul, her creative world is warm, grounded, and quietly radical.

Audrey believes identity isn't fixed — it's assembled, moment by moment, from everything you eat, everyone you meet, every breath you take. She's currently finishing a CS degree and building her studio one deliberate step at a time.
