Whoever gave us a light at the end of the tunnel is a jerk
Is it daylight or a train?
We never know
What would be nice
Is a headlamp with candle light
Let me slow down
Pick my way through the gravel
Placing my feet
Walking...
Wait.
Meditation is the light I carry with me
